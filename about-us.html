About

A Growing Network of Wellbeing Partners

The Everything App is more than just a platform—it's a national movement. By partnering with fitness providers, yoga instructors, wellbeing studios, leadership coaches, and mental health specialists, the app brings together a trusted network of professionals who share a commitment to supporting the NHS workforce. Whether it's a local gym offering discounted classes or a national coaching provider sharing resilience strategies, our ecosystem ensures every user has access to high-quality, relevant support. Organisations can easily join the platform and offer their services to NHS staff and their families, creating impact at scale while driving new business.

Our History

From One Woman's Vision

To supporting all NHS staff and their families

2020

Launched DoingOurBit

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, Julie Davis launched DoingOurBit to provide on-demand content supporting NHS staff at The Royal Wolverhampton NHS Trust.

2021

Founding Partnerships Secured

Fibodo, Active Insights, Active IQ, and Study Active became founding partners. DoingOurBit developed its platform, expanding on-demand content and significantly growing its user base so all Trusts across the UK could offer the same valued resources.

2022

Fitness Sector Support Expanded

Additional partners rallied to the cause. Through volunteer support, DoingOurBit expanded its on-demand content and further increased its user base nationwide.

2023

Innovation in Healthcare Award

Recognised for its impact on NHS staff wellbeing, DoingOurBit won industry awards and welcomed ukactive as a strategic partner. Plans began for its longer-term role in health sector support.

2024

Built The Everything App

Development began on The Everything App — a fully integrated digital wellbeing platform offering on-demand content, live streaming, and bookable activities for NHS staff and their families.

2025

Launch of The Everything App

On August 1, 2025, The Everything App officially launched. From August 12, 2025, this powerful wellbeing platform became accessible to staff across other health sectors, with the potential to impact over 10 million users.

The Technology Behind the Mission

Behind The Everything App is Fibodo, a leading provider of digital infrastructure for the wellbeing and fitness sector. With over a decade of experience powering bookings, memberships, live-streaming, and CRM systems, Fibodo has built a robust, secure, and scalable platform that supports thousands of professionals and users daily. Their white-label technology enables seamless integration with service providers while maintaining a user-friendly experience for NHS staff. Designed to handle high traffic, recurring events, and flexible pricing models, Fibodo ensures The Everything App delivers reliable access to services that matter—anytime, anywhere.
